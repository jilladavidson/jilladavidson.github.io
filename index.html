Experienced locum vet

Jill is a practicing veterinary surgeon with over 30 years of experience, qualifying from The University of Edinburgh in 1981. Originally from Aberdeen, Jill likes to combine her love of travel with her work and is available for locum work across the UK as well as abroad*.

Jill specialises in small animals however she also enjoys seeing equine practice, having worked with horses throughout both her professional and personal life. Her love of animals and enjoyment of her work has seen her run Pet Blood Bank Donations for the Midlands region over a number of years, lead training courses for nurses with mulitple providers, and act as President for the East Midlands Branch of the BVA. She is an advocate for embracing new technologies and is always searching for ways to improve clinical processes.

*Subject to country registration requirements. Currently registered to practise in the UK only.




Small Animal Surgery

  • All routine surgeries completed on a regular basis, including: Neutering, caesarians, splenectomies & cystotomies
  • Most soft tissue surgery, wound repair & reconstruction, entropion and other eye surgeries
  • Basic orthopaedic surgery
  • Recently completed a surgical courses in brachycephalic and renal surgery


Opthalmology, Dematology & Medicine

  • Particular interest in opthalmology and dermatology
  • Experience treating most medical cases including diabetic and other endocrinological cases
  • Medical management of complex hospitalised cases
  • Trained and competent in ultrasonography


Practice Management

  • Comfortable working autonomously or collaborating in larger teams
  • Significant experience running single vet practices as a locum
  • Own VDS cover
  • RCVS CPD records current and compliant
